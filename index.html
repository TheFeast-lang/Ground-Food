Welcome to Green Earth

Gardening enriches our lives by connecting us to nature, promoting well-being, and contributing to a more sustainable, healthy environment in Northern Bukidnon State College.

Only garden in areas designated by the campus authorities to prevent disruption to other activities or spaces.

Choose native or low-maintenance plants that suit the local climate and won't harm the ecosystem or become invasive.

Water plants responsibly to conserve water, and avoid over-watering, which could cause damage or waste.
