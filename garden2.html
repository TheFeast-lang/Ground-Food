Welcome to Garden 2

Bok Choy

Health Benefits: good source of potassium, a mineral that helps regulate blood pressure by balancing the negative effects of sodium. The fiber and antioxidants in bok choy also contribute to lowering cholesterol and improving overall heart health.

Green Chilli

Health Benefits: Green chilies are an excellent source of vitamin C, an essential antioxidant that boosts the immune system, promotes healthy skin, and supports wound healing.

Green Beans

Health Benefits: Green beans are a good source of vitamin C, vitamin K, vitamin A, and folate. These vitamins support immune function, skin health, and tissue repair, among other functions.

Squash

Health Benefits: Squash, especially varieties like butternut squash and zucchini, are rich in vitamin A (from beta-carotene), vitamin C, vitamin B6, and folate. These vitamins support various bodily functions, including immune health, skin health, vision, and energy production.
